You are designing a premium landing page for "Legal Ninja" - a competitive gamified law quiz platform for Nigerian students preparing for bar exams and university finals.

## PROJECT CONTEXT

Product: Legal Ninja (Nigerian EdTech + Gaming Platform)
Target: Law students 18-28 preparing for bar exams and finals
Current Design: Cyberpunk neon theme with ninja/samurai aesthetic (works but needs enhancement)
Goal: High-conversion landing page maintaining gaming energy with premium polish

## CURRENT STATE ISSUES TO FIX

❌ Long page requires excessive scrolling
❌ Vague value proposition ("master the law through competitive duels")
❌ Missing social proof (no testimonials/reviews)
❌ No feature showcase explaining what makes it unique
❌ Guest option competes with sign-up CTA
❌ Limited trust indicators
❌ Mobile experience cramped

## DESIGN SECTIONS (Hero → Footer)

1. STICKY NAVIGATION
   - Logo left | Nav links center (Features, How it Works, Pricing, About) | Sign In / Sign Up right
   - Mobile: Hamburger menu
   - Transparent, becomes solid on scroll

2. HERO SECTION (Full-width, above fold)
   Layout: Split screen preferred (60% left, 40% right)

   LEFT SIDE:
   - Clear headline (benefit-driven, not "master through duels")
   - Supporting subheading (1-2 sentences)
   - Two CTAs: Sign Up (primary cyan), Sign In (secondary ghost)
   - Trust badge: "Trusted by 10K+ Nigerian Law Students"

   RIGHT SIDE:
   - Enhanced animated ninja illustration OR gameplay animation
   - Floating elements showing: XP badges, level progression, ranking

3. VALUE PROPOSITION (3-column grid, responsive)
   Show 6 benefits as cards:
   ⚡ Competitive Gaming - Battle in duels, track rank, earn XP
   📚 Exam-Aligned Questions - Past exams, bar prep, AI scenarios
   🎯 Smart Learning - Weak area detection, daily challenges, spaced repetition
   🏆 Instant Feedback - Real-time scoring, detailed explanations
   👥 Community Features - Study groups, leaderboards, friend duels
   💎 Premium Unlocked - Unlimited questions from ₦700/week

4. HOW IT WORKS (Timeline or horizontal steps - 4 steps)
   1. Sign Up → 2. Pick Subject → 3. Answer & Learn → 4. Track Progress
   Each with icon, title, description, and app mockup screenshot

5. FEATURES SHOWCASE (3 alternating blocks)
   Block 1 - Quiz Modes: Solo Practice, Duels, Battle Royale, Daily Challenges, Mocks
   Block 2 - Question Types: MCQ, Essay AI-scoring, Past papers, Topic filtering
   Block 3 - Analytics: Dashboard, weak area detection, streaks, levels, leaderboard

6. SOCIAL PROOF (Testimonials)
   3 student testimonial cards: Photo | Name & Track | 2-line quote | 5-star rating | Result
   Example: "Went from 40% to 92% in 3 months"

7. PRICING SECTION
   Free: 100 free questions, all modes, daily challenges, community
   Premium: Unlimited questions, advanced analytics, priority support (₦700/week)
   Show both tier cards with clear CTAs

8. FAQ (Accordion)
   5-7 questions covering: Free questions limit, bar exam prep, past papers, mobile app, ranking, cancellation, security

9. FOOTER
   Logo + copyright | Quick links (Privacy, Terms, Help) | Social links (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram)

## COLOR PALETTE (Cyberpunk Neon - maintain)

Primary Colors:
- Cyber Cyan: #00F5FF (Primary CTA, accents)
- Cyber Purple: #C026D3 (Secondary, premium)
- Cyber Green: #22FF88 (Success, progress)
- Cyber Gold: #FFD700 (Highlights, VIP)
- Cyber Red: #FF0055 (Errors)

Backgrounds:
- Dark: #0f0f1e (Primary)
- Card: #1a1a2e
- Text: #e2eaf0 (primary), rgba(226,234,240,0.45) (muted)

## TYPOGRAPHY

- Headings: Space Grotesk Bold/Black (H1: 48-64px desktop, 32px mobile)
- Body: Space Grotesk Regular 16px
- Monospace: Space Mono (for stats/badges)

## DESIGN STYLE

- Modern gaming platform aesthetic (Discord, Valorant, League energy)
- Cyberpunk neon with subtle gradients
- Glass-morphism effects: rgba(26,26,46,0.4) + blur(10px)
- Glow effects: 0 0 20px rgba(0,245,255,0.3)
- Smooth animations on scroll/hover (0.6s, easeInOut)
- Floating particles in background
- Parallax scrolling on hero
- Neon borders with subtle glow

## RESPONSIVE DESIGN

- Desktop: 1440px+ (full features)
- Tablet: 768px-1439px (2-column where appropriate)
- Mobile: 320-767px (stacked, simplified animations, 44px touch targets)

## INTERACTIVE ELEMENTS

- Primary buttons: Solid cyan bg, glow, 1.05 scale on hover
- Secondary buttons: Ghost style, subtle hover fill
- Forms: Glowing border on focus, cyan outline
- Accordions: Smooth expand, icon rotate on open
- Smooth scroll to sections

## ACCESSIBILITY (WCAG 2.1 AA)

- Color contrast minimum 4.5:1 for text
- Keyboard navigation support
- ARIA labels for icons
- Visible focus indicators
- Alt text for all images
- Semantic HTML
- Lighthouse score target: 85+

## KEY REQUIREMENTS

✅ Mobile-first responsive design
✅ High conversion focus (clear value prop, strong CTAs)
✅ Premium polish while maintaining gaming energy
✅ Showcase unique features (duels, leaderboards, weak area detection)
✅ Build trust (social proof, security, credibility)
✅ Fast loading (optimize images, GPU animations)
✅ Maintain ninja mascot as key brand element
✅ Clear free vs premium messaging (no aggressive upselling)

## DESIGN INSPIRATIONS

- Discord (community energy, clean UI)
- Duolingo (gamification, conversion funnels)
- Valorant (premium gaming aesthetic)
- Modern EdTech platforms (Coursera, Skillshare)

## SUCCESS METRICS

Target: 8-12% conversion rate (sign-up/visit)
Desired: 2+ min time on page, <40% bounce rate, 100 mobile score, 85+ Lighthouse
